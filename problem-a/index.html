Raya

Raya is the main character from the Disney movie Raya and the Last Dragon.

To save her kingdom from Druun monsters that convert people into stones, Raya the main character, embarks in a journey. To find harmony and bring her dad back who was turned into stone, she finds the last dragon, Sisu. Towards the end of the movie Raya who has trust issues, learns the meaning of friendship and trust.
